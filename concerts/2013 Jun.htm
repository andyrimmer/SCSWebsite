15th June 2013 ~ St Barnabas church


"Absolutely fantastic! It was great to hear such a wide range of music sung by such a good choir. The requiem and Dyson in D were particularly excellent"

Featuring:

Mozart: Requiem
Bruce Montgomery: Spring the Sweet Spring,
Full Fathom Five, My Joy, My Life, My Crown!
D. L. Sayers: I've got a Little List
Herbert Howells: St. Hugh's Galliard
George Dyson: Revielle
Franz Schubert: An die Musik
Geoffrey Bush: 'Prélude' and 'Sicilienne' from Suite Champêtre
George Dyson: Evening Service in D
Charles Wood: Expectans Expectavi
A. L. Peace: Hymn 699: O Love That Will Not Let Me Go

Murder, Morse & Mozart

Duncan Saunderson conductor

Sarah Dacey, soprano
Daniel Laking, countertenor
David Lee, tenor
Tom Edwards, bass
Julian Littlewood, piano & organ

with the Summertown Players
