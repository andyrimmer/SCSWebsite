Come and Join Us!

Summertown Choral Society gives two concerts a year, generally in January/February and May/June, in North Oxford venues. The year consists of two terms, and runs from early September to May or June, with short breaks for Christmas and Easter.

Joining SCS

Summertown Choral Society welcomes new members of all ages, voices, and abilities. There is no audition. Just come along and sing with us in a rehearsal before deciding whether you'd like to join. Rehearsals for our next concert begin on Thursday 6th September 2018; we will be registering new members for several weeks after this.

Programme for the 2018-2019 season

Winter concert: 9 February 2019 at St Andrew's Church, Linton Road: Monteverdi's Vespers of 1610.

Summer concert: 15 June 2019 at St Andrew's Church, Linton Road: Schubert's Mass in E flat (D950), with music by Beethoven, Mozart, and Joseph and Michael Haydn.

More information can be found on the Next Concert page.

Rehearsal time and venue

Rehearsals take place on Thursday evenings from 8.00-9.30pm in the Church of St Michael and All Angels, Summertown.

Membership fees

Membership costs for the 2018-19 season are £120 for the year or £65 per term. This includes music hire. The annual subscription is waived for those under 30 and is half-price for full-time students over the age of 30, and may be waived or reduced for members in financial hardship.

Please contact our membership secretary if you have any queries.
