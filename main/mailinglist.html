Subscribe to our mailing list

To receive updates by email about future Summertown Choral Society concerts and events, please subscribe to our mailing list. You will receive an email in advance of each concert (two per year), which will include a link to Tickets Oxford which can be used to purchase tickets. If you tick the box to say that you are interested in singing with us, you will also receive an email prior to the new choir term containing information about trying out and joining SCS.

We will not use your contact details for any other purpose, and we will not pass them on to any other organisation.
