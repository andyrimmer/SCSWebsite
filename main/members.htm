Practice aids

For learning parts:

Summertown Choral Society subscribes to the John Fletcher music website, which provides rehearsal files in a variety of formats to help singers learn their parts. All current SCS members who wish to use the site are registered for it and have their own individual log-ins. If you encounter problems, or if you previously opted out and would now like to use the site, please contact our Group Owner, Marie Bridge, at jfletcherrep@summertownchoral.org.uk.
